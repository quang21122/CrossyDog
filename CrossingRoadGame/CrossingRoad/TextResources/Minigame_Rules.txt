MINIGAME
- Your objective in this minigame is collecting all the coins visible, further increasing your points.
- If the main character is hit during the minigame, the game will be considered over.
- No save/load available in the minigame, but player can still pause the game at anytime.
- The player has 50 seconds to complete the minigame. 
- If the main character is alive after 50 seconds or cross the finish line, the player will successfully finish the game.
